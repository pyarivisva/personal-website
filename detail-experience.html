Read & Share

A desktop application, Read & Share, designed to manage and track book donations efficiently, supporting library collections and donor records. Built using OOP in Java with NetBeans and MySQL, it securely organizes data on book inventories, donor information, and donation history. This system provides librarians an intuitive platform for recording donations, monitoring inventory, and retrieving donor records, enhancing library management efficiency.

Baki

This mobile app is designed with a user-friendly UI/UX to facilitate food transactions, aimed at reducing food waste through easy access to surplus food offers and donations. Users can quickly browse food offers from restaurants and stores, complete with descriptions and expiration dates. The app connects users with local charities for food donations, ensuring surplus food reaches those in need while promoting a community focused on sustainability.

First Aid

This mobile app features a thoughtfully designed user interface and experience aimed at guiding users in providing first aid during emergencies. With intuitive navigation, users can quickly access essential emergency instructions, a dedicated section for first aid lessons, and emergency contact numbers. The app also includes a chatbot for immediate assistance and answers to common questions, empowering users to respond promptly in emergencies and potentially saving lives.

Internet of Things Project

The IoT project focuses on creating a comprehensive system that leverages relays, Real-Time Clocks (RTC), various sensors, buttons, and a Telegram bot to monitor and control devices efficiently. The relays enable automation of connected devices, allowing users to control them remotely. Sensors will collect environmental data, which can trigger actions or notifications via the Telegram bot, enhancing user engagement. This combination of technologies offers a robust solution for managing diverse devices in a seamless and automated manner.
